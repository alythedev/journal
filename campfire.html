campfire dubai my beloved • march 2026

In November 2025, Hack Club announced Campfire — their next satellite game jam that would run in 200+ cities around the world in late Feb 2026.

So.. I signed up to organise one in Dubai.

✴︎ the calm before the storm

The two months leading up to Campfire Dubai were a buzz. I was sending emails to venues, sponsors, and attendees; designing posters, banners, and Instagram posts; coordinating with our merch supplier; and doing so much more.

I learnt soo much throughout the whole process too! I had absolutely no idea how to do half the things I listed above before this.

My favourite thing to work on might've actually been our merch. My friend Zeina made the amazing designs, and for a while I was terrified we wouldn't be able to actually order them in time.

Campfire took over my life for those 2 months, but it was so worth it. We ended up landing the Microsoft MEA Hedquarters as our venue, and had a few really cool sponsors. We were also aiming for 50 attendees (which icl I didn't think would happen) and had 95 sign ups before the event!?

✴︎ the storm

The day of the event finally came around and.. people actually showed up, I had a team I could rely on, and our venue was stunning.

It couldn't be more perfect.
